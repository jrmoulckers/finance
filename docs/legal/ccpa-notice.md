## 2. Sensitive Personal Information

To the extent Finance processes information that is considered **sensitive personal information** under California law, we use it only for limited purposes such as:

- Providing the financial tracking service you requested
- Authenticating you and protecting your account
- Detecting, preventing, and investigating security incidents or fraud
- Debugging and maintaining service reliability
- Complying with legal obligations

Finance is not designed to use or disclose sensitive personal information to infer characteristics about you, and it does not sell or share sensitive personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising.

## 3. Business and Commercial Purposes for Collection

We may collect, use, or disclose personal information for the following business or commercial purposes:

- Providing Finance’s core budgeting, transaction tracking, goal tracking, and related features
- Creating and administering accounts
- Authenticating users with passkeys or other supported sign-in methods
- Syncing data across your devices if you choose to enable sync
- Supporting shared-household and collaboration features you choose to use
- Protecting the security, integrity, and availability of Finance
- Preventing abuse, fraud, and unauthorized access
- Debugging, troubleshooting, and improving app stability
- Processing optional analytics or crash reports only when you have opted in
- Complying with legal obligations and responding to valid requests from regulators, courts, or law enforcement

## 5. Your California Privacy Rights

Subject to applicable exceptions, California residents may have the right to:

- **Know / Access** the categories of personal information we collect, the sources of that information, the business purposes for using it, the categories of recipients to whom we disclose it, and the specific pieces of personal information we hold about you
- **Delete** personal information we collected from you
- **Correct** inaccurate personal information
- **Opt out of sale or sharing** of personal information, although Finance currently does not sell or share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising
- **Limit the use or disclosure of sensitive personal information** to the extent that right applies; Finance is designed to use sensitive personal information only for permitted service, security, and compliance purposes
- **Data portability**, including receiving exported data in a usable format where required
- **Non-discrimination**, meaning we will not deny goods or services, charge different prices, or provide a different level or quality of service because you exercised your privacy rights

You may designate an authorized agent to submit a request on your behalf where permitted by California law.

If you use an authorized agent, we may require:

- Written authorization signed by you, unless the agent has valid power of attorney
- Proof that the agent is authorized to act for you
- Verification of your identity directly with you, where permitted by law

## 8. How We Verify Requests

To protect your privacy and security, we may need to verify your identity before fulfilling a request. Depending on the request, we may verify you by:

- Asking you to sign in to your Finance account
- Confirming control of the email address associated with your account
- Requesting additional information reasonably necessary to verify the request

We will only use verification information for the purpose of handling your request.

## 9. Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information

Finance does **not** sell personal information.

Finance does **not** share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising.

Because Finance does not sell or share personal information in this way, there is currently no separate opt-out workflow to stop a sale or advertising share. If our practices change, we will update this notice and provide any required opt-out methods.

Finance also does not knowingly sell or share the personal information of minors under 16.

## 10. Non-Discrimination

We will not discriminate against you for exercising your California privacy rights. That means we will not deny you service, charge you different prices, or provide a different level or quality of service just because you made a privacy request.

## 11. Financial Incentives
